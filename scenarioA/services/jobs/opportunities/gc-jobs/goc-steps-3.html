Candidate assessment

When you apply for a job with the public service of Canada, you may be required to write a test or series of tests throughout the selection process. br> Below you will find the complete list of Public Service Commission tests often used to assess candidates. For each test, you will find more information about:

  • what each test assesses
  • the format of each test
  • how each test is administered

Note that when you click on a specific test, you will be redirected to the Public Service Commission Web site for more detailed information.
