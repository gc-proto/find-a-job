Career streams

Career Streams are databases for candidates with college or university specializations. Managers contact you if the skills and experience in your application match job openings they are trying to fill.

Education and Non-Canadians

If you were educated outside of Canada, you must have your certificates and/or diplomas assessed.

Non-Canadians living in Canada are welcome to apply to the PSR. If your citizenship status changes to Canadian citizen after applying, please update your GC Jobs applicant account.
