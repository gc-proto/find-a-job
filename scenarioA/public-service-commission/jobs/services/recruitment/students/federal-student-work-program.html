Federal Student Work Experience Program

Get valuable work experience as a student in the federal public service. We offer a wide variety of jobs across Canada. Opportunities include field work, research, administration, IT, finance, trades, policy, communications and many more.

You must be a full-time high school, CEGEP, college or university student, and you must be returning to full‑time studies in the next academic year.

Job matching

Apply to our inventory and you will receive an email notification when you have been matched with a job opportunity.
